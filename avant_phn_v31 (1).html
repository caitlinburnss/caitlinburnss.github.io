💡 How to identify consulting vs. delivery tenders

  • Consulting signals: "capacity building", "practice support", "evaluation", "QI", "system design", "workforce support", "program coordination", "planning", "consulting"
  • Delivery signals (skip): "service delivery", "clinical services", "psychological therapies", "treatment", "nursing", "GP services", "allied health treatment"
  • Check the eligibility: If it says "registered health professional" or "approved provider" in eligibility criteria — it is clinical delivery, not consulting
